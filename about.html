About AlbumViz

AlbumViz is a creative visualization tool that transforms music into art. By mapping audio frequencies to colors, AlbumViz creates posters, gradients, and digital artwork that allow you to see your music.

The algorithm uses a logarithmic frequency-to-color mapping inspired by how humans perceive both sound and light. Low frequencies shift toward warmer colors, while higher frequencies are represented by cooler tones, producing a natural and immersive translation of sound.

AlbumViz was originally developed by Cameron Noval as a blend of physics, art, and coding. Today it serves as both an art gallery and a tool for musicians, educators, and fans who want to experience music in a new way.
